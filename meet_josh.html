I'm a New York-based world traveler, art director and designer who builds pretty things and kickass playlists. My background runs the gamut of advertising and marketing, from strategy to production, which gives me a unique perspective on getting things done.

Reaching beyond just aesthetics with experience in copywriting, strategy, media and project management, I match versatility, efficiency and creativity to produce quality design under even the most grueling deadlines.

When I'm not at my computer, you can catch me exploring the city, traveling the world, pondering my next tattoo (5 total), challenging my body, obsessively consuming music or just taking it all in with my incredible friends and family.

WANDERLUST

Since I can remember, the world and its surrounding nature has fascinated me. It begain as an obsession with animals growing up and quickly developed into complete wanderlust by the time I landed in Australia during my college study abroad program. In the years after, I've been committed to a goal of experiencing every continent on this amazing planet. A goal with a finish line in sight as I close in on my 35th birthday in two years, and a trip to my 7th continent: Africa.
